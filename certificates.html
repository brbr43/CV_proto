Volunteer & Humanitarian Certificates

Recognition for community service and cultural event organization in Syria

🤝 Humanitarian Work

Omraha Volunteer Team — فريق عمرها التطوعي

Certificate from Omraha Volunteer Team (فريق عمرها التطوعي), a community volunteer organization, recognizing humanitarian work during the 2023 earthquake in Syria.

Served as an effective team leader, coordinating relief efforts and remaining on the ground for days assisting affected communities — helping displaced families and supporting ongoing aid operations.

📚 Cultural Organization

Ministry of Culture — Syria Book Fair

Certificate from the Ministry of Culture for participating in organizing Syria's largest book exhibition (معرض الكتاب) — the most significant cultural book fair in the country.

Contributed to the planning and coordination of the event, helping deliver a major cultural experience for thousands of visitors and supporting the promotion of reading and knowledge across Syria.
